About

TELECOM WOMEN CENTRAL ORGANIZATION

Telecom Women's Central Organisation is a registered society under societies of registration Act XXI of 1860. It came into being in January 1986 and presently, its headquarter is HM 8, Harishchander Mathur Lane, Connaught Place, New Delhi-1.

The wife of incumbent CMD, BSNL generally remains as President of this organization and other office bearers viz. Secretary, Joint Secretaries, Treasurers are elected members of TWCO.

Wives of the officers of DOT, TEC, BSNL, MTNL,TCIL and the working women officers of these organizations can become member of TWCO and participate in its various activities. This body works for the welfare of the family and members of the telecom employees.

"KALAMANJUSHA" an online art exhibition has been launched on 06th of November, 2023 by TWCO in collaboration with TWWOs.

TWCO, a non-profitable welfare organization was formed on January, 1986 with an objective of bringing together the families of telecom fraternity(DOT,MTNL,BSNL & TCIL). The organization since its inception has been rendering immense services to thousands of women employees and their families and our endeavours have contributed significantly in women empowerment.

TWCO has been organizing activities like training programme for women, free health check-up camps, beauty care center, tuition classes and other social activities for the welfare of women employees and their family.

TWCO, with headquarters at HM8, Harishchandr Mathur Lane, Connaught Place, New Delhi-1 has the following aims and objectives for the benefit of family members of the telecom employees.

01

To promote, encourage and coordinate the work for the economic uplift, mutual assistance, self-reliance and general well being of the Telecom. Families specially women and children.

02

To foster a spirit of friendship, unity and social under standing among members, with a view to encounter talent among the Telecom employees and their families.

03

To promote artistic, cultural, economic and social activities among members, with a view to encounter talent among Telecom employees and their families.

04

To provide assistance in deserving cases to the families of the Telecom. Employees in matters of adult literacy education, family welfare, medical care, prevention of diseases and various social obligations.

05

To strive for improvement of working conditions and office environments for women employees of the Department.

06

To provide and to assist the Department to provide for rest and recreational facilities both in offices and residential colonies.

07

To organize institutional support to working women and their children by setting up of crèches, tailoring central hostels community centers schools, children’s clubs, balwadis and other institutions.

08

To disseminate information regarding social property and legal rights of women and to arrange counseling services.

09

To render assistance towards national causes and to provide relief in the event of national calamities, independently or in conjunction with other non-Telecom Social Welfare Organizations.
